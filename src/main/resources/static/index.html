About Us

Pet Haven was founded in 2011 by Sarah Nobel and Robin Ross. The two first met at University of Louisiana at Lafayette in the Ragin Rescue. Through the laughter, fun, friendship, and a love pets Pet Haven was born. We believe every animal deserves a safe, loving environment. Our mission statement derived from the love and care that originally founded the shelter.

Our mission at Pet Haven is to provide a safe, caring, and nurturing environment for all animals in need. We strive to prevent animal cruelty and suffering, and to promote the humane treatment of animals through education, awareness, and advocacy

We believe that every animal deserves to be treated with dignity, respect, and compassion. We are dedicated to improving the lives of animals in our community and beyond, and to creating a world where all animals are valued and protected.

By supporting Pet Haven, you are making a positive impact in the lives of these animals and contributing to our mission of giving them a second chance at a happy and healthy life. We thank you for your generosity and for joining us in our efforts to make a better world for all creatures.
